Oranges

Oranges are a citrus fruit known for their bright orange color, juicy flesh, and tangy-sweet flavor. They are rich in vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants, making them a nutritious and refreshing choice. Oranges are enjoyed fresh, juiced, or used in a variety of culinary applications around the world.

Originating in Southeast Asia, oranges have been cultivated for thousands of years and are now grown in warm climates across the globe. There are many varieties, including Navel, Valencia, Blood orange, and mandarins, each with unique characteristics and uses.
