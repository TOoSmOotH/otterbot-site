Apple Facts

More than just a fruit

🍎

Botanical Interest

Apples belong to the rose family (Rosaceae) and come in over 7,500 varieties worldwide.

🍐

Nutritional Value

Packed with fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants. One medium apple has about 95 calories.

🍏

Varieties

From tart Granny Smith to sweet Honeycrisp, there's an apple for every palate.

🍋

Storage

Keep apples in the fridge to extend freshness. They can last up to 6 weeks when stored properly.
